# Join Our Team

We are actively looking for highly motivated candidates to join the **AI-IOT Lab** at RBCCPS, IISc. 

Our research lies at the intersection of **Cyber-Physical Systems (CPS)**, **Internet of Things (IoT)**, and **Data Science**, with a strong focus on energy sustainability and smart building applications.


## Current Openings

### 1. Project Staff / Research Associates
We frequently look for project staff to assist with ongoing research and development. 
**Ideal candidates should have:**
* Strong programming skills in **Python**.
* A Bachelor's or Master's degree in CS, ECE, or related fields.

### 2. PhD & M.Tech Research
If you are already admitted to IISc (or planning to apply), we welcome you to discuss potential research topics.

### 3. Postdoctoral Fellows
We welcome applications from prospective postdoctoral fellows with a strong publication record in CPS, Data Mining, or Energy Analytics.


## How to Apply

If you are interested in working with us, please send an email to **Prof. Pandarasamy Arjunan**.

**Email:** [samy@iisc.ac.in](mailto:samy@iisc.ac.in)

**Please include the following in your email:**
1.  **Subject Line:** Please use a clear subject line (e.g., *"Application for Project Staff Position - [Your Name]"*).
2.  **CV/Resume:** A detailed CV highlighting your education and skills.
3.  **Portfolio/Code:** A link to your GitHub profile or portfolio website is highly recommended.
4.  **Statement:** A brief explanation of why you are interested in our lab and how your skills align with our research themes.
